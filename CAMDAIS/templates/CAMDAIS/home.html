Our Mission

Our mission at the Mathematical Anxiety Website is to empower individuals to overcome their mathematical anxiety and develop a positive relationship with mathematics. We are dedicated to providing a supportive and inclusive online platform where individuals of all ages and backgrounds can access resources, guidance, and a community to conquer their fears and unlock their full potential in mathematics.

Our Vision

Our vision for the Mathematical Anxiety Website is to create a world especially in our country where mathematical anxiety is understood, acknowledged, and effectively addressed, enabling individuals to embrace mathematics with confidence, curiosity, and joy.
